Digital Check-In Reporting (AXP)

A single view of guest adoption, performance and friction across the check-in journey

Three Questions We Answer
1
How many guests are using digital check-in?
2
How well does it perform (speed, success vs failure)?
3
Where are bottlenecks by brand, property or step?
What We Measure
Adoption & Volume
Total arrivals vs digital check-in usage, trends over time by property/brand
Completion & Failure
Status breakdown (completed / failed / abandoned), first failed step to pinpoint friction
Timing & Behaviour
Lead time before arrival, number of attempts per booking, first-time success rate
Historical Report
Daily refresh. Trend analysis, optimisation & benchmarking across markets
Live Report
Near real-time. Day-of-arrival monitoring & rapid issue detection
Check-In Duration & Step-Level Insight
Total time from first attempt to completion with median/average toggle. Time per step (per attempt & per booking) to identify slow or complex steps. Handles multi-step & legacy page formats.
Reach & Campaign Effectiveness
Total Arrivals
Received Email
Started
Completed
Conversion & drop-off at each stage. Dynamic step completion chart (required vs optional steps).
Segmentation & Filters
Property-level performance with volume filters. Segment by market, rate, source, nights. Badge/loyalty tier filtering. Kiosk vs pure online separation.
